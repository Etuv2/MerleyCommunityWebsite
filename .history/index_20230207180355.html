About Us

We are a vibrant and welcoming community center located in Wimborne. Our mission is to provide a safe and inclusive space for people of all ages and backgrounds to come together and enjoy a variety of activities. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone. Our staff and volunteers are dedicated to providing a safe and supportive space for all members of our community. We invite you to come and explore all that our centre has to offer. Whether you’re looking for a place to socialize, learn something new, or just have some fun, we’re here to help. Thank you for visiting our website and we look forward to seeing you soon!
